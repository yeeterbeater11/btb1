1. About Beat the Bet

Beat the Bet is an Australian-based gambling addiction recovery and support platform. It is operated by an Australian sole trader. All enquiries may be directed to beatthebetadmin@gmail.com. Further business details are available upon reasonable request.

2. Important Disclaimer

Beat the Bet is a peer-support and recovery platform — not a medical, counselling, crisis, financial, or legal service. It is not a substitute for professional help. The platform may surface crisis resources where distress is detected; this does not constitute clinical assessment or intervention.

If you are in crisis, contact emergency services immediately.

3. Information We Collect

We collect: account information (email, username, password); recovery content (journal entries, timer data, milestones, chat messages, photos); usage and activity information; device and technical information; and user-generated community content.

4. Why We Collect Information

To provide and improve the Services; personalise your recovery experience; maintain community safety; detect fraud and unauthorised access; comply with legal obligations; and collect anonymised analytics for platform improvement. We do not sell this data or share it for advertising.

5. Community Chat Moderation

Community messages may be automatically screened using keyword detection and reviewed by human moderators. Messages violating Community Guidelines may be removed. Chat messages are automatically deleted after 30 days as a data minimisation measure.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored via Supabase on Amazon Web Services infrastructure in Sydney, Australia (ap-southeast-2). We use encryption in transit, secure authentication, access controls, and monitoring. No method of storage is completely secure.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — authentication, database, storage
  • Amazon Web Services — infrastructure via Supabase
  • Last.fm API — music recommendations (artist names only, no personal data)
  • Vercel — hosting and deployment

8. Cookies & Local Storage

We do not use advertising cookies. We use localStorage for session persistence, user preferences, and platform functionality. Disabling local storage may affect app functionality.

9. Push Notifications

We may provide optional push notifications for recovery reminders, payday alerts, milestone celebrations, and progress updates. You can enable or disable them through your device settings. Notification content is not shared with third parties.

10. Data Retention

User data is retained while your account is active. When deleted, personal information is removed within a reasonable period. Community chat messages are deleted after 30 days automatically.

11. Your Privacy Rights

Australian users have rights under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) including access, correction, and deletion. You can delete your account in the app. Unresolved complaints may be escalated to the OAIC at www.oaic.gov.au. GDPR rights apply where applicable.

12. Children's Privacy

Beat the Bet is for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When material changes are made, we will notify users through the app or other appropriate means.

14. Contact Us

For privacy questions, requests, or complaints: beatthebetadmin@gmail.com. Further business details are available upon reasonable request.

15. Consent & Lawful Processing

By using the Services you consent to collection and use of your information as described here. Some processing is on the basis of legitimate interest (security, moderation, fraud prevention). You may object by contacting beatthebetadmin@gmail.com.
